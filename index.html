Capabilities
Everything in one agent

Deploy once. The PArAsYtE agent silently propagates through your environment and reports back — giving you full visibility and control from a single dashboard.

🛡️
Endpoint Detection & Response
Real-time process monitoring, credential dump detection, lateral movement alerting, and one-click kill/isolate/purge actions.
🖥️
Remote Machine Management
MeshCentral-powered RMM with full remote shell, file transfer, hardware inventory, and software deployment across all platforms.
🔐
Secure Communications
End-to-end encrypted comms channel between agents and the command server. Zero plaintext in transit, ever.
📊
DevOps Monitoring
Prometheus metrics, Loki logs, Tempo traces — full observability stack with Grafana dashboards baked in for your Kubernetes workloads.
🔔
Push Threat Alerts
Critical threats push directly to your device — MALWARE, SUSPECT, and INFO severity with one-tap kill and investigate actions.
📱
Native PWA
Installable on iOS, Android, and desktop. Offline-capable, push-enabled, works standalone — no app store required.
